ARTICLE 1: COCKFIGHTING AND RELATED EQUIPMENT

Sections
   12-1.1   Prohibited
   12-1.2   Gaffs or slashers prohibited
   12-1.3   Violation—Penalty
§ 12-1.1 Prohibited.
   It is unlawful for any person to engage or participate in any cockfighting exhibition.
(Sec. 13-2.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 7, Art. 1, § 7-1.1) (Am. Ord. 01-58)
§ 12-1.2 Gaffs or slashers prohibited.
   It is unlawful for any person to manufacture, buy, sell, barter, exchange, or have in such person’s possession any of the implements commonly known as gaffs or slashers, or any other sharp instrument designed to be attached in place of or to the natural spur of a gamecock or other fighting fowl.
(Sec. 13-2.2, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 7, Art. 1, § 7-1.2)
§ 12-1.3 Violation—Penalty.
   Any person violating this article shall be punished by a fine of not less than $250 and not exceeding $1,000 or by imprisonment not exceeding 30 days, or by both.
(Sec. 13-2.3, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 7, Art. 1, § 7-1.3) (Am. Ord. 01-58)
