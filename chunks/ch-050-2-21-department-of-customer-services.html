ARTICLE 21: DEPARTMENT OF CUSTOMER SERVICES

Sections
   2-21.1   Municipal Reference and Records Center
§ 2-21.1 Municipal reference and records center.
(a)   There shall be a municipal reference and records center in the department of customer services.
(b)   It shall be the duty of the director of customer services to guide the development and maintenance of a collection of publicly released documents, which may be made available to any officer or employee of the city government, and to maintain an electronic depository for the purpose of facilitating public access.
(c)   The municipal reference and records center is designated as a depository for publications issued by or for city agencies. Each city agency shall, immediately upon release of a publication, deposit with the municipal reference and records center a copy or copies of the final version of any consultant study, document, compilation, journal, report, and rules in a manner, quantity, and format as determined by the municipal reference and records center, but excluding publications determined by the issuing agency to be of a confidential nature.
(Added by Ord. 18-30)
