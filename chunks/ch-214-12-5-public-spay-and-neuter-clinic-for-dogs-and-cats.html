ARTICLE 5: PUBLIC SPAY AND NEUTER CLINIC FOR DOGS AND CATS

Section
   12-5.1   Authority for clinic and fees
   12-5.2   Administrative rules
§ 12-5.1 Authority for clinic and fees.
   The city is authorized and empowered to establish a clinic, through a fee-for-service contract, at which members of the public may have dogs and cats spayed or neutered in a humane manner.
(Sec. 13-39.1, R.O. 1978 (1987 Supp. to 1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 7, Art. 5, § 7-5.1) (Am. Ords. 92-72, 95-34, 03-14, 10-1, 24-31)
§ 12-5.2 Administrative rules.
   The director of customer services shall adopt administrative rules pursuant to HRS Chapter 91 for the purposes of implementing, administering, and enforcing this article, including but not limited to setting the fees for spay and neuter services.
(Added by Ord. 24-31)
Editor's note:
   *This ordinance takes effect on July 1, 2025; provided that § 12-5.1, as it read on the day prior to July 1, 2025, shall remain in effect until such time that the Director of Customer Services has adopted rules pursuant to § 12-5.2, and such rules have taken effect.
