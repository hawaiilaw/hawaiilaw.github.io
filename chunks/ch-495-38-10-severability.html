   38-10.1   Severability
§ 38-10.1 Severability.
   This chapter, as enacted by this ordinance, is declared to be severable. In accordance therewith, if any portion of the chapter is held invalid for any reason, the validity of any other portion of this chapter shall not be affected and if the application of any portion of this chapter to any person, property, or circumstance is held invalid, the application hereof to any other person, property, or circumstances shall not be affected.
(Sec. 30-6.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.) (Sec. 29-5.1, R.O. 1978 (1987 Supp. to 1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 28, Art. 10, § 28-10.1)
