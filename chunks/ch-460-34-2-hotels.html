ARTICLE 2: HOTELS

Section
   34-2.1   Register requirements
§ 34-2.1 Register requirements.
(a)   Every owner, keeper, or proprietor of any hotel shall keep a register in accordance with HRS Chapter 486K.
(b)   The owner, keeper, or proprietor shall at all times make the register available for inspection by the chief of police upon the chief’s request.
(c)   A violation of subsection (a) shall be subject to punishment in accordance with State law. A violation of subsection (b) shall be subject to a fine of $200, imprisonment for up to 30 days, or both.
(Sec. 13-11.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 41, Art. 4, § 41-4.1) (Am. Ord. 01-09)
