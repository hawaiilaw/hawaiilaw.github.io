§ 1-15.1 Definitions.
   For the purposes of this article, the following definitions apply unless the context clearly indicates or requires a different meaning.
City. Includes the departments and agencies of both the executive and legislative branches of the City and County of Honolulu.
Forms. Refers to all city issued documents to be filled by a noncity person, organization, or entity requesting the provision of a city service or the issuance of a city license or permit. The term does not refer to such documents after they have been filled out by any person, organization, or entity.
Online. Refers to being connected to the Internet or World Wide Web.
(1990 Code, Ch. 1, Art. 15, § 1-15.1) (Added by Ord. 13-29)
§ 1-15.2 Access to city forms.
(a)   The city shall make all city forms available online for electronic filing or downloading.
(b)   The city shall recognize and accept all properly filled in downloaded forms, provided that the form is accompanied by all necessary payments and specified supplemental materials.
(1990 Code, Ch. 1, Art. 15, § 1-15.2) (Added by Ord. 13-29)
