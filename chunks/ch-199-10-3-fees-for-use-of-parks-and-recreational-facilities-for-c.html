§ 10-3.1 Commercial scuba diving and snorkeling.
   Scuba diving and snorkeling activity:
(a)   Annual permit: $900;
(b)   Monthly permit: $75; and
(c)   Daily permit: $10.
(Sec. 27-2.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 10, Art. 3, § 10-3.1)
