§ 2-11.1 Powers, duties, and functions.**
   The fire chief shall be charged with the prevention of fires and the protection of life and property against fire and shall:
(1)   Report losses. Report all fire losses periodically to the mayor;
(2)   Maintain equipment. Be responsible for the maintenance and care of all property and equipment used by the fire chief’s department; and
(3)   Fire extinguishers. Grant, withhold, suspend, or revoke certificates of fitness authorizing persons to repair, fill, or refill portable fire extinguishers.
(Sec. 2-11.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 2, Art. 11, § 2-11.1)

§ 2-11.2 Additional powers, duties, and functions of the fire chief.
   The fire chief shall:
(1)   Identify and assess the training, qualifications and certifications necessary for personnel assigned to rescue companies, establish additional recommended standards as necessary, and upon completion of the assessment, update the city position descriptions to be aligned with the rescue companies' best practices;
(2)   Formalize a long-term training plan to effectively implement any necessary additional standards and ensure consistency in standards among all personnel assigned to rescue companies, and provide this plan to the council and fire commission within 60 days of November 23, 2020;
(3)   Ensure that all personnel assigned to existing rescue companies have, or are in the process of obtaining, the requisite training, qualifications, and certifications to be rescue company personnel; and
(4)   Staff additional rescue companies only with personnel that have the requisite training, qualifications, and certifications to be rescue company personnel.
(Added by Ord. 20-39)
