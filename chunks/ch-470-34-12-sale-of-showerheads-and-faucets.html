ARTICLE 12: SALE OF SHOWERHEADS AND FAUCETS

Sections
   34-12.1   Sale of certain showerheads and faucets prohibited

§ 34-12.1 Sale of certain showerheads and faucets prohibited.
   Effective April 12, 1994, only showerheads and kitchen faucets that conform to the requirements of the Energy Policy Act of 1992, P.L. 102-486, and only lavatory faucets that conform to the requirements of the plumbing code shall be offered for sale in the city.
(1990 Code, Ch. 40, Art. 9, § 40-9.1) (Added by Ord. 93-77)
§ 34-12.2 Enforcement—Penalty.
(a)   The department of planning and permitting shall enforce this article and may adopt rules pursuant to HRS Chapter 91 for the administration and enforcement of this article.
(b)   Any person violating this article shall be fined not less than $100 and not more than $250 for each violation.
(1990 Code, Ch. 40, Art. 9, § 40-9.2) (Added by Ord. 93-77)
