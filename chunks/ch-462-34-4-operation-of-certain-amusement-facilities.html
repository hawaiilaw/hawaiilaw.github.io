ARTICLE 4: OPERATION OF CERTAIN AMUSEMENT FACILITIES

Section
   34-4.1   Skating rink—Certain hours of operation prohibited—Penalty
§ 34-4.1 Skating rink—Certain hours of operation prohibited—Penalty.
(a)   No skating rink shall do business or operate on any day between the hours of 12:00 midnight and 8:00 a.m.
(b)   Any person violating this section shall, upon conviction, be punished by a fine not exceeding $1,000.
(Sec. 13-18.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 41, Art. 7, § 41-7.1)
