ARTICLE 7: EXITS

Section
   16A-7.1   Exits
§ 16A-7.1 Exits.
   Every dwelling unit or guest room shall have access directly to the outside or to a public corridor. All buildings or portions thereof shall be provided with exits, exitways, and appurtenances as required by the building code.
(Sec. 25-7.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 27, Art. 7, § 27-7.1) (Ree. Ord. 97-50)
