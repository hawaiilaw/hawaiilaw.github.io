ARTICLE 18: TRANSPORTATION FUND

Sections
   6-18.1   Creation
   6-18.2   Purpose
§ 6-18.1 Creation.
   There is created and established a special fund to be known as the “transportation fund.”
(Sec. 5-23.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 6, Art. 18, § 6-18.1) (Am. Ord. 20-19)
§ 6-18.2 Purpose.
   The transportation fund is for the following purposes:
(1)   For city bus, handi-van, and rail system purposes, including:
(A)   The management, operation, and maintenance of the city bus, handi- van, and rail system; and
(B)   The pro rata share of the expenses of the department of transportation services attributable to the administration of the city bus, handi-van, and rail system;
(2)   As a depository for all revenues generated by or received from the city bus, handi-van, rail system, and parking fees derived from the joint traffic management center parking garage.
(3)   To provide for expenses of operation, maintenance, improvement, and betterment of the joint traffic management center parking garage facilities.
(Sec. 5-23.2, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 6, Art. 18, § 6-18.2) (Am. Ords. 91-27, 97-02, 12-36, 20-19)
