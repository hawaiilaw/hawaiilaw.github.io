ARTICLE 25: COMMEMORATIONS AND CELEBRATIONS

Sections
   1-25.1   ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i Month
   1-25.2   Honor Our Kūpuna Month
§ 1-25.1 ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i Month.
   The month of February is known and designated as ‘Ōlelo Hawai‘i Month to celebrate and encourage the use of Hawaiian language. This month is not to be construed as a city holiday.
(Added by Ord. 22-9)
§ 1-25.2   Honor Our Kūpuna Month.
   The month of May is hereby known and designated as “Honor Our Kūpuna Month” to honor the great wisdom, generational knowledge, and immense contributions of the beloved kūpuna of O‘ahu. This month is not to be construed as a city holiday.
(Added by Ord. 24-25)
