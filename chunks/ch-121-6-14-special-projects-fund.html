ARTICLE 14: SPECIAL PROJECTS FUND

Sections
   6-14.1   Creation
   6-14.2   Purpose
§ 6-14.1 Creation.
   There is created and established a special fund to be known as the “special projects fund.”
(Sec. 5-18.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 6, Art. 14, § 6-14.1)
§ 6-14.2 Purpose.
   All moneys received under special contracts entered into by and between the city and the State, such as for maintenance of State highways, and all moneys received from various other sources for operating and capital improvement expenses for which no financing has been provided in other funds, such as contributions from property owners exclusive of improvement district assessment collections, shall be deposited into the special projects fund and expended for the purposes authorized.
(Sec. 5-18.2, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 6, Art. 14, § 6-14.2)
