ARTICLE 9: TERMINATION OF WATER SERVICE

Section
   43-9.1   Authorization
§ 43-9.1 Authorization.
   It has been determined that termination of water service to enforce payment of sewer service charges is necessary and that termination of water service to industrial users for violations pursuant to § 43-5.7(b) may be necessary. Therefore, the board of water supply is given the authority to terminate water service for delinquency in payment of sewer service charges or pursuant to § 43-5.7(b) when so directed by the director.
(Sec. 11-9.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 14, Art. 9, § 14-9.1) (Am. Ord. 94-73)
