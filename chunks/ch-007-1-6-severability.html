ARTICLE 6: SEVERABILITY

Section
   1-6.1   General provisions
§ 1-6.1 General provisions.
   If any provision of the Revised Ordinances of Honolulu, or the application thereof to any person or circumstances, is held invalid, the remainder of the Revised Ordinances of Honolulu, or the application of the provision to other persons or circumstances, shall not be affected thereby.
(Sec. 1-6.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 1, Art. 6, § 1-6.1)
