ARTICLE 11: SEVERABILITY

Section
   16A-11.1   Severability
§ 16A-11.1 Severability.
   If any part of this code is for any reason declared unconstitutional or invalid, the other separable parts thereof shall not be affected thereby.
(Sec. 25-12.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 27, Art. 11, § 27-11.1) (Am. Ord. 97-50)
