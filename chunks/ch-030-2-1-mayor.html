ARTICLE 1: MAYOR*

Sections
   2-1.1   Election and term of office
Editor’s note:
   *In general, see Charter Art. V, Chap. 1.
§ 2-1.1 Election and term of office.
   Except for the filling of a vacancy in the office of the mayor as provided by Charter § 5-106, the electors of the city shall elect a mayor whose term of office shall be four years beginning at 12:00 meridian on the second day of January following the mayor’s election.
(Sec. 2-1.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 2, Art. 1, § 2-1.1)
