ARTICLE 3: HIGHWAY FUND*

Section
   6-3.1   Redesignation
§ 6-3.1 Redesignation.
   The special fund designated as “road fund” is redesignated “highway fund” pursuant to HRS § 249-18, as amended.
(Sec. 5-4.1, R.O. 1978 (1983 Ed.)) (1990 Code, Ch. 6, Art. 3, § 6-3.1)
Editor’s note:
   *Establishment, see HRS § 249-18, as amended.
