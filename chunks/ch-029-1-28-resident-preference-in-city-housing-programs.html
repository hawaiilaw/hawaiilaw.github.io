ARTICLE 28: RESIDENT PREFERENCE IN CITY HOUSING PROGRAMS

   1-28.2   Definitions

§ 1-28.1 Purpose.
   This article establishes a preference for city residents who participate in various housing programs offered by the city, to the extent allowed by law.
(Added by Ord. 25-5)
§ 1-28.2 Definitions.
   For the purposes of this article, the following definition applies unless the context clearly indicates or requires a different meaning.
   City Resident. Any individual who has a stated intent to reside or be domiciled in the city.
(Added by Ord. 25-5)
§ 1-28.3 Preference policy.
   It is the policy of the city to ensure that every program or initiative that promotes or is intended to promote the provision of housing, and that was created, developed, implemented, administered, required, supervised, managed, or guided by the city must, to the greatest extent allowed by law, include a preference for city residents. This policy instills only a preference for city residents and does not exclude non-city residents from such programs or initiatives. This preference must be construed broadly to effectuate the intended purpose of installing the aforementioned preference.
(Added by Ord. 25-5)
§ 1-28.4 Rules.
   The department or agency designated by the mayor to implement this article may adopt rules pursuant to HRS Chapter 91 for the implementation, administration, and enforcement of this article.
(Added by Ord. 25-5)
